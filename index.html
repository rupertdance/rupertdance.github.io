Hi. I'm Rupert.

I make digital things for people. I write code. I try to work on things that matter.


What I can do, and what I have done.

I architect. The Big Picture. What problem are we trying to solve? How does it fit into the world? Does it even need to exist?

I set up the back end. Domains, a CDN for speed, make it HTTPS. Hosting, usually AWS. Databases, MariaDB on EFS. Containerisation, Docker, Kubernetes, Helm.

I build and develop. The data structures. A CMS, headless or Drupal 7/8. A node.js app. Serverless, Lambda! Or just a static site, Vue / React. It must be fit for purpose. As simple, faste and secure as possible while meeting all needs. A brochure site? SquareSpace. Selling a lot? Shopify, themed. Subscriptions and auto-renewing payments? Stripe.

I implement web designs. The front end. HTML, LESS/SASS, JS. Give me logos in SVG, fonts, designs, and make them work. Flexbox is great, Bootstrap can be handy too.

I make things usable. Nothing is sadder than good work gone unused. People need training, products need a champion. Code needs maintenance.

Most importantly I keep learning. I don't know everything. There are so many tools to get things done. The examples I list above are the ones I've used and enjoyed. If you keep learning about new tools, and learning about users, you'll do well.


What I know about, but what I am not.

I know design. But I'm not a designer. But I've been lucky enough to work closely with some amazing people who do. I've learned a lot.

I know security. But I'm not an cyber security specialist. But I've been lucky enough to work on very secure projects, maintain a SecureDrop server, and I know the value of 2FA and the right IAM roles.

I know words. But I'm not an editor or a copy writer. But I've been lucky enough to work closely with some amazing people who are. I know how important they are.
